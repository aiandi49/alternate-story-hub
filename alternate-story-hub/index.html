How It Works

1

Write or Record a Script

Any spoken word, short story, or narrative script. Timestamped or plain text — both work. Drop it in scripts/.

2

Open the Generator

Go to src/generator.html — paste your script, give it a title and genre tag, then click Generate.

3

Claude Builds the Story

The AI reads your script and generates 10 branching scenes, 4 choices each, with A/B style endings — all in one dark-theme HTML file.

4

Save & Publish to Hub

Download the generated HTML to stories/, add a card to index.html, and push to GitHub. It's live.
